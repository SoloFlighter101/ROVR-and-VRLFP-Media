How to edit:
When you open photoshop see bottom right then you can find Layers menu,
all editing process in this tutorial will be conducted in this menu. 

How to change the text:

1. Click on the group "Text" in Layers menu to show the entire text.
2. Select the text you want to edit by double clicking it. 
3. After you double click on it the text on the design will be highlighted,
then click on the highlighted text to change the text as you desire.
