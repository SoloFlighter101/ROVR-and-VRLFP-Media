Thank you for downloading our mockups. We hope you enjoy our design resources. Using our mockups is very simple, just follow these 3 simple steps:

1. Open the PSD file in Adobe Photoshop.

2. Open the Layers panel and double-click on the Smart Object layer.

3. Now, replace the sample design with your own design. To get the best result, make sure you match the colors and size of sample graphic with your graphic.

4. Finally, save the file.  
Now, you are all done. Congratulations!
